Our Features

Whatsaf provide you with many useful features like...

Message Timer

Experience precision in messaging with Whatsaf's Message Timer feature. Schedule your messages effortlessly, ensuring they reach recipients at the optimal moment. Perfect for reminders, and time-sensitive communications. Take control of your messaging timing with Whatsaf.

Online Status

Stay in the loop with Whatsaf's Online Status Messenger feature, alerting you when contacts come online. Seamlessly connect with friends or seize business opportunities at the perfect moment. Be informed and responsive in real-time, enhancing your communication experience. Whatsaf keeps you connected, making online interactions more dynamic than ever.

Occasional Wisher

Never miss a special moment with Whatsaf's Occasional Wisher feature. Effortlessly send heartfelt birthday wishes, anniversary greetings, and more, all at the perfect time. Customize your messages to make every occasion memorable, ensuring your connections feel valued and appreciated. With Whatsaf, celebrate life's milestones with the touch of a button.

Vanish Mode

Experience privacy like never before with Whatsaf's Vanish Mode feature. Engage in confidential conversations knowing that messages will automatically disappear for both parties after the chat ends. Whether sharing sensitive information or having casual discussions, Vanish Mode adds an extra layer of security to your communication. Enjoy the freedom of ephemeral messaging with Whatsaf.

Chat Sentiment

Elevate your communication with Whatsaf's Chat Sentiment feature. This intelligent tool analyzes the tone of your recent chats and sends personalized email summaries, keeping you connected and informed. Receive tailored insights based on your conversations, fostering a deeper understanding of your interactions. With Whatsaf, communication goes beyond the chat, providing valuable insights through thoughtful email summaries.

Channels Comment Section

At Whatsaf, we've reimagined the way you engage with WhatsApp channels. Introducing our dedicated comment section, where you can seamlessly post comments on channel posts. This feature enhances collaboration and community interaction, allowing for real-time feedback and discussions. Elevate your group communication experience with Whatsaf's intuitive comment section, promoting lively conversations and meaningful connections within your WhatsApp channels.
